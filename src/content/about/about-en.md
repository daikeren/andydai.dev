I'm Andy Dai, CTO and co-founder at [Codeer.ai](https://codeer.ai), where we're building a no-code AI Agent platform.

Every day I write code, make product decisions, and figure out where AI actually helps — and where it doesn't. This blog is where I share those lessons.

## What I write about

- **AI Tool Reviews** — Evaluating tools from a builder's perspective. Not benchmarks, but real decisions: what worked, what didn't, and why.
- **Engineering Team × AI** — How AI changes the way teams build software. Workflows, code review, and the human side of adoption.
- **Startup Builder's Log** — Tech choices, product traps, and things I learned the hard way at Codeer.

## My take

Most AI content tells you what to use. I'd rather help you figure out *whether* to use it.

I'm skeptical by default — not because AI isn't useful, but because adopting it without asking the right questions first is how teams waste months instead of saving them.
